JAKE MALLIA

These are the mock-ups and poster for a live case assignment for the National Blood Transfusion Service.
The design idea came from looking around my house for random objects to see if I could somehow
relate them to blood donation. I came across a piggy bank and linked it to my project by the fact
that a piggy bank and blood bag are both used to "save". So I replaced a coin with a drop of blood.
I used Adobe Illustrator and Photoshop to create this.
