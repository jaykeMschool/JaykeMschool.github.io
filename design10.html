JAKE MALLIA

This is an illustration I made for a live case assignment that was projected in Valletta in Christmas time.
I was tasked to design 2 Christmas themed illustrations, this is the second one. It is designed to resemble
a snowglobe with 2 sides. One side consisting of santa claus waving with a christmas tree and large
candy cane next to him and then a side with the grinch and a burning christmas tree and a broken
candy cane next to him. I them used Adobe Aftereffects to make a spinning transition effect on the snowglobe.
I used Adobe Illustrator and Aftereffects to create this.
